Our Mission & The Tournament's Heart

In the heart of every swing, putt, and cheer at Mandi's Pink Tribe Golf Tournament sponsored by Contractors Risk Management, is the indomitable spirit of Mandi. A beacon of hope, courage, and unwavering determination, Mandi's journey with breast cancer ignited a flame that has since sparked a community-wide movement towards making a difference. Fueled by Mandi’s infectious energy and her dream of a world without breast cancer, our mission is to gather our community through the joy of golf. Each swing, each putt, isn't just a game; it's a strike against breast cancer, raising funds and awareness for the American Cancer Society’s critical work. Mandi's leadership transforms our collective effort into a powerful force of hope and progress. This year marks the beginning of a thrilling new chapter for a cherished tradition. For over 17 years, this golf tournament has been a cornerstone event in our community, dedicated to the fight against breast cancer. Today, under the vibrant banner of Mandi's Pink Tribe, we embrace the legacy of these gatherings while steering our collective efforts toward a new horizon. With Mandi at the helm, the spirit of this event is reinvigorated, promising an even greater impact. As we tee off for the first time under the banner of Mandi's Pink Tribe, every player, sponsor, and supporter becomes a foundational pillar of this renewed legacy. Your participation carries forward a proud tradition of community, compassion, and competition, now amplified by a renewed commitment to making every swing, every cheer, and every moment count. Together, we're not just participants in a tournament; we're pioneers in a movement poised to make a monumental impact in the fight against breast cancer. Step into Mandi’s vibrant tribe and join us on the green for a day of exhilaration, camaraderie, and meaningful action. Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or new to the course, your presence amplifies our chorus of hope and determination. Inspired by Mandi’s journey and driven by our shared vision, let’s swing, celebrate, and conquer breast cancer together, one victory at a time.

Experience the Excitement: What to Expect at the Tournament

Prepare to be part of an unforgettable day filled with camaraderie, competition, and cause. From the first tee to the final putt, discover what makes the Mandi's Pink Tribe Golf Tournament sponsored by Contractors Risk Management, a must-attend event for players and supporters alike. Here’s a sneak peek into the day’s highlights.
Champion the Course Tackle the picturesque Apple Hill Golf Club, a course that challenges and delights in equal measure. Feel the rush of competition amidst breathtaking views.
  • Navigate 18 holes designed to test your skills and strategy.
  • Compete for a range of prizes in various categories – there’s something for everyone!
  • Enjoy real-time scoring and live leaderboards for an immersive golfing experience.
Swing with Purpose Every stroke contributes to our fight against breast cancer. Join hands with Mandi’s Pink Tribe in making a tangible difference through sport.
  • Be part of our inaugural event and help set a precedent for generosity and engagement.
  • Your participation directly supports the American Cancer Society’s breast cancer initiatives.
  • Wear pink to show solidarity and be part of a powerful visual statement on the course.
More Than a Game Engage in a day filled with more than just golf. From enjoyable local food to exciting entertainment, there’s a wealth of experiences waiting for you.
  • Savor a curated selection of foods and beverages, keeping you energized from start to finish.
  • Connect with fellow participants, forming new friendships and networking opportunities.
  • Partake in a silent auction featuring unique items and experiences, with proceeds going to our cause.
