Welcome to Mandi's Pink Tribe

Mandi's Pink Tribe is dedicated to raising funds for the American Cancer Society's breast cancer fundraising efforts through our annual golf tournament fundraiser and by organizing a team for the Making Strides for Exeter NH event. Join us in making a difference in the fight against breast cancer.

Mandi's has an amazing story of her own journey through breast cancer and has shared it with the world during her 3rd Making Strides of Exeter, NH
It was recorded by her husband and we are delighted to share her moving speech here.

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
When is the annual golf tournament held?The annual golf tournament is held in June at Apple Hill Golf Club.
How can I participate in the golf tournament?You can participate in the golf tournament by signing up as a player or a team. Sponsorship opportunities are also available.
What is the Making Strides for Exeter NH walk fundraiser?The Making Strides for Exeter NH walk fundraiser is a charity walk event held in September to support the American Cancer Society's breast cancer research.
How can I join Mandi's Pink Tribe for the walk fundraiser?You can join Mandi's Pink Tribe for the walk fundraiser by signing up as a participant or creating a team to fundraise together.
How can my organization become a sponsor for Mandi's Pink Tribe fundraisers?Your organization can become a sponsor for Mandi's Pink Tribe fundraisers by contacting us through the website and exploring sponsorship opportunities.
Who is this years title sponsor for the Golf Tournament?For 2026 we are still looking for our title sponsor.  If you are interested, please feel free to reach out.  It is a great opportunity to get involved and help a great cause.
