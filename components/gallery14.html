Laughs for a Cause: The 3rd Annual Comedy Night at Chunky’s

Join us for the 3rd annual Comedy Night at Chunky's Cinema in Manchester, NH, as we bring together three incredible comedians for a night of laughter and support for Making Strides Against Breast Cancer. Headliners Annie Powell, Stacy Kendro, and Matt Barry will take the stage to deliver their unique, side-splitting performances in a night full of entertainment. This annual event has become a beloved tradition, combining top-tier comedy with a great cause, helping our Exeter community fight breast cancer. Get ready for a night you won’t forget!
Annie Powell Headlining our 3rd annual Comedy Night at Chunky's, Annie Powell brings her signature sharp wit and energetic style to the stage in support of Making Strides Against Breast Cancer. A crowd favorite at Mohegan Sun Casino, Annie has shared the spotlight with some of the biggest names in comedy. Known for her hilarious takes on everyday life, her fast-paced humor will keep you laughing all night long. Expect an evening of laughter that supports a cause close to all of us in Exeter.
Stacy Kendro Next up, Stacy Kendro brings her bold, acerbic humor to Comedy Night at Chunky’s, offering a refreshing, unfiltered view of the world. A staple of the New York comedy scene, she’s performed at legendary venues like the Friar’s Club and has opened for legends like Joan Rivers. Stacy’s fearless “tell it like it is” style has earned her a spot among the best, and she’s ready to deliver a night of unforgettable comedy, all while supporting Making Strides Against Breast Cancer.
Matt Barry Matt Barry, a powerhouse in the New England comedy scene, rounds out the headliners for our 3rd annual Comedy Night at Chunky's. Known for his relatable, quick-hitting humor, Matt has earned titles like “New England’s Best Bar Comic” and has performed alongside legends such as Tom Green and Gilbert Gottfried. His mix of wit and charm will have you in stitches, all while supporting the vital work of Making Strides Against Breast Cancer here in Exeter.
